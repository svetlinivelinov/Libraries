Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

Session duration varies by service:

  • Natural/Everyday Look: 45-60 minutes
  • Special Event/Party: 60-90 minutes
  • Bridal Makeup: 90-120 minutes
  • Editorial/Fashion: 2-3 hours

We recommend scheduling extra time for consultations and touch-ups.

Yes! We offer on-location services within a 30-mile radius. Travel fees apply based on distance:

  • 0-10 miles: No additional charge
  • 10-20 miles: $25 travel fee
  • 20-30 miles: $50 travel fee

For destinations beyond 30 miles, please contact us for a custom quote. We also have a fully equipped studio available for appointments.

We use only professional, high-quality makeup brands including:

  • Foundation & Base: Giorgio Armani, Charlotte Tilbury, Fenty Beauty
  • Eyes: Urban Decay, Anastasia Beverly Hills, Pat McGrath
  • Lips: MAC, Tom Ford, Dior
  • Special Effects: Kryolan, Mehron, Ben Nye

All products are hypoallergenic and regularly sanitized. We can accommodate sensitive skin and specific brand preferences upon request.

We recommend booking as early as possible to secure your preferred date and time:

  • Bridal Services: 3-6 months in advance
  • Special Events: 2-4 weeks in advance
  • Regular Sessions: 1 week in advance

However, we often accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability. Contact us to check our current schedule!
